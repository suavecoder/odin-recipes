Best Mexican Dish Ever

Description

Carne con chile is the best meal anyone can eat in the world. It is often cooked different ways, but i loved mine to be cooked with pork ribs. The ribs are braised then slowly cooked in a chili sauce

Below is a list of ingredients required to make this dish

  • tomatoes
  • pork ribs

carneconchile

Directions

  1. Braise Pork
  2. Blend garlic and chile in blender
  3. Place prok in chili and simmer
