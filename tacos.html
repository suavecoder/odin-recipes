Bomb Taco Dish

Description

Tacos are a staple mexican food dish. Below, you'll learn how to cook carnitas tacos

Here is a list of ingredients required to make this dish

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Salt

Carnitas Tacos

Directions

  1. Season Pork Shoulder
  2. Place Pork shoulder in crockpot for 8 hours
  3. Shred pork and add to tortillas
  4. Add your favorite toppings like onions or salsa
